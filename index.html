How It Works

TraceON turns your environment into a real-world detective hunt. Start a story online, follow clues outdoors, scan QR codes to unlock the next steps, and solve mysteries solo or with friends.

Features

Outdoor Adventure

Engage with your surroundings while solving immersive stories. No screen-only gameplay.

Solo or Team

Play alone or collaborate with friends. Customize your adventure style.

Immersive Stories

Each game presents a unique narrative that unfolds as you progress through clues.

QR Clues

Scan real-world QR codes to unlock the next part of the story. Discover the city and the mystery simultaneously.
